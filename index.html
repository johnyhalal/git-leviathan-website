GitLeviathan

A cross-platform desktop GUI for Git.

Download for macOS Apple Silicon · free

Requires Apple Silicon (M1 or newer). Windows and Linux builds are on the way.

Clone from anywhere

Connect your GitHub and GitLab accounts, or paste any Git URL to clone a repository in seconds.

Native desktop app

A fast, focused window that lives on your Mac — no browser tab, no distractions, just your repositories.

Light & dark themes

A clean interface that follows your system appearance, or switch between light and dark whenever you like.

First launch on macOS

The app isn't code-signed yet, so macOS Gatekeeper may warn that it's from an unidentified developer. Right-click the app and choose Open once, or clear the quarantine flag: 

xattr -dr com.apple.quarantine /Applications/GitLeviathan.app
